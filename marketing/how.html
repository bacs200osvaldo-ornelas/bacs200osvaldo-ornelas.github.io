How ECA Works

  • ECA is partnered with Aims Community College.
  • Students can only enroll when they are in 9th or 10th grade.
  • Classes are held 5 days a week, each for 55 minutes.
  • Once students graduate with their diploma and AA degree, the credits will transfer to whatever university students go to next.
