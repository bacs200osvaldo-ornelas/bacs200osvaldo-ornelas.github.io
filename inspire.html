Michael Oher

Everyone has their own favorite hero that they take inspiration from. This can be a fictional character in a movie who saves others with their superpowers, or it can even be a real human being. It is their values and actions that shape them as a hero.

The following characteristics below are what I believe shapes a hero.

  • Motivation - They motivate others to follow their dreams, no matter how impossible it may seem
  • Determination - They never give up regardless of their situation
  • Entrepreneurial - They show confidence to continue their goals even if they initially fail
  • Humility - Even though someone may become very good at something, they know they are not better than others and still put respect

Personally, Michael Oher is a great example of all of these characteristics, making him one of the top people I look up to. He was a hero for himself. Coming from a rough childhood, to becoming very successful. Even though it seemed impossible for him to succeed in his early life, he never gave up and ultimately it paid off.
